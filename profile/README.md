# ChannelAssist

**Incent and motivate your reps and partners to deliver Sales Channel ROI.**

ChannelAssist is a SaaS platform and professional-services company for **channel incentive management** — helping organizations engage indirect sales partners and representatives through configurable rewards, training, gamification, and claims management.
